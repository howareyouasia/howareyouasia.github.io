About Us

"How Are You" is a Singapore based organization that tries to bring the issues of mental health to the forefront.

Educate

We provide courses and workshops for different caregivers and beneficiaries.

Eradicate

We normalise the topic by bringing it into daily touchpoint and context.

Empower

We build strong communities with our partners and users.

Ecosystem

We create a self sustaining mental well-being support system.
